Xuan Jiang, Ph.D.

I am an Assitant Professor in the Department of Economics at Jinan University since Fall 2022. I received my Ph.D. in Economics at Purdue University in 2018. Before joining Jinan, I was a postdoctoral scholar at The Ohio State University. My research interests are in labor economics, economics of education, and science of science. My research focuses on understanding human capital production through education in every stage of the lifecycle and its intersection with gender issues.
